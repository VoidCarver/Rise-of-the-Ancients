Update 1.5.2 BETA

General

  • Villagers
    Repair rate for buildings increased by 10%
    (Ship repair rate unchanged).

Technologies

  • Battle Axe
    Research time decreased from 45 sec -> 40 sec (Default 40 sec)
    Now also grants Axeman +0.05 Move Speed
  • Tool Age Leather Armor Upgrades (Infantry, Archers & Cavalry)
    Research time decreased from 45 sec -> 40 sec (Default 30 sec)
  • Stone Mining
    Upgrade cost changed from 120 Food 50 Stone -> 100 Food 50 Wood (Default 100 Food 50 Stone)
    Stone Miners work 20% faster and carry +2 stone (Default 30% +3 carry)
    +1 Slinger attack/range
  • Stone Quarry
    NEW – Bronze Age – Market – All Civs
    Upgrade Cost: 150 Food 100 Wood, 75 sec
    Stone Miners work 20% faster and carry +2 stone
    +1 Slinger attack/range
  • Siegecraft
    Stone Miners work 20% faster and carry +2 stone (Default 30% +3 carry)
    +1 Slinger attack/range
    Villagers have bonus attack vs. towers/walls
  • Logistics
    Now also grants Swordsman line +0.05 Move Speed
  • Legionary
    Now grants +60 HP instead of +40 HP (already in Patch)
    No longer grants +0.05 Move Speed

Units

  • Slinger
    Cost increased from 40 Food 10 Stone -> 45 Food 10 Stone
    Base Range increased from 4 -> 5
    Base Line of Sight increased from 6 -> 7
    Base Damage decreased from 2 -> 1
    Base Pierce Armor decreased from 3 -> 2
    (Unchanged +3 Bonus damage vs. Archers/Walls/Gates/Towers).
  • Chariot Archer
    Base Range decreased from 7 -> 6

Buildings

  • Barracks
    Cost increased from 125 Wood -> 150 Wood
  • Small Gate
    Base Pierce Armor increased from 1 -> 2
    Armor Class 13 – Stone Walls & Gates & Towers decreased from 0 -> -1
  • Medium Gate
    Base Pierce Armor increased from 2 -> 3
    Armor Class 13 – Stone Walls & Gates & Towers decreased from 0 -> -1
  • Fortified Gate
    Base Pierce Armor increased from 3 -> 4
    Armor Class 13 – Stone Walls & Gates & Towers decreased from 0 -> -1
