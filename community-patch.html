Currently Live - Update 1.4

Technologies

  • Needlework
    NEW - Tool Age - Town Center
    Villagers +15 HP and +1 Attack
    Cost 50 Food, Research Time 20 sec
    (Available to every Civilization except Sumerians).
  • Battle Axe
    Upgrade research time increased from 40 sec -> 45 sec
  • Leather Infantry Armor
    Research time increased from 30 sec -> 45 sec
  • Stone Mining
    Upgrade cost increased from 100 Food 50 Stone -> 120 Food 50 Stone
  • Composite Bowman
    Upgrade research time reduced from 100 sec -> 80 sec
  • City Watch
    Now available to every Civilization.
    Research cost changed from 100 Food 15 sec -> 75 Food 20 sec
    (Previously not available for Assyrians, Egyptians and Persians).
  • Urbanization
    Now available to every Civilization.
    (Previously not available for Choson, Hittites, Lac Viet, Minoans, Phoenicians and Yamato).
  • Zealotry
    Hit Points bonus decreased from 40 -> 30
    Melee attack bonus damage decreased from 7 -> 6
    (decreased values based on Needlework values to retain original Zealotry balance).

Units

  • Clubman
    Train time increased from 26 sec -> 30 sec
  • Slinger
    Cost increased from 40 Food 10 Stone -> 45 Food 10 Stone
  • Bowman
    Projectile Speed decreased from 8 -> 7
    (only affects Bowman without Alchemy upgrade).
  • Scout
    Cost decreased from 90 Food -> 85 Food
    Now have x2 conversion resistance
  • Cavalry, Heavy Cavalry and Cataphract
    Cost changed from 70 Food 80 Gold -> 75 Food 70 Gold
  • Legionary
    Move Speed increased from 1 -> 1.05
  • Scythe Chariot
    Blast Damage decreased from 1 -> 0.8 (100% -> 80%).
  • War & Armored Elephant
    Move Speed increased from 0.75 -> 0.80
  • Heavy Catapult
    Pierce Armor reduced from 5 -> 4
    Blast Radius reduced from 1.5 -> 1.4
  • Ballista & Helepolis
    Projectile Speed increased from 4.067 -> 4.9

Buildings

  • House
    Hit Points increased from 75 -> 100
  • Towers
    Now cause 100% more damage to Scout Ship-line and Transport Ship-line
    (Affected units are Scout Ship, War Galley, Trireme, Light Transport and Heavy Transport).
  • Ballista Tower
    Reload Time decreased from 3.1 -> 3.0
    Projectile Speed increased from 4.067 -> 4.9
    (New values are equal to Ballista Tower stats in Aoe1 DE).

Civilizations

  • Assyrians
    Archers fire 22/33% faster in Tool/Bronze Age
    (Previously "Archers fire 11/22/33% faster in Tool/Bronze/Iron Age").
  • Greeks
    Academy units move 25% faster and cost -20%
    (Previously "Academy units move 30% faster and cost -20%").
  • Lac Viet
    Military Units created 20% faster starting in Tool Age
    (Previously "Military Units created 20% faster").
    (Default "Military Units created 25% faster").
  • Macedonians
    Archery Ranges work 10% faster (New TB)
    (Previously "Houses +50 HP").
  • Shang
    Government Centers work 80% faster (44% less time) (New TB)
    (Previously "Town centers provide +4 population room").
  • Sumerians
    Villagers have +1 Attack in Tool Age.(New Civ Bonus)
    (do not have access to Needlework Technology).
  • Yamato
    Scouts cost -10% Cavalry and Horse Archer lines cost -15%
    (Previously "All cavalry units and Horse Archers cost -15%").
