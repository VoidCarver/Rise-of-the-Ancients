"Work Factors" (WF) Overhaul on Farm's gathering rates

  • To be clear, we did not add this "Work Factor Adjustments", they are present in the game since release. (This overhaul is mandatory since we're changing move speed in Tool and Wheel speed otherwise Farm's gathering rates would go all over the place).
  • But why are those "WF" here?
    Well, in AOE DE, Farmers are standing still and farming on the same tile, in an AOE2 Farming Cycle, Villagers are gathering a quarter of there carry capacity on each 4 randomly chosen tiles.
  • Devs needed to adjust Farmers to the AOE2 Farming mechanic and at the same time, probably wanted to retain AOE DE Farming balance, so they ended up adding those Work Factors so Villagers move speed doesn't impact gathering rates while farming, only specific Farming bonuses (Minoans) or Extra Work Rate (Palmyrans) do.
  • Villagers still benefit from being faster the further away they are from drop off location but not in the Farming cycle exactly like it's working on AOE DE.
  • A lot of efforts was put into those adjustments to make sure everything is working as intended.
